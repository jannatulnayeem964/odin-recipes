Brown Butter Chocolate Chip Cookie

Description

Brown Butter is so gooood!! It elevates the cookie!

Hershey chocolate might be a little too sweet feel free to use your favorite chocolate. I still liked hershey tho.

Ingredients

  • 7 tablespoon of unsalted butter
  • 5 tablespoon of salted butter
  • 1/2 cup of jaggery/gur
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 1 whole egg
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla bean paste
  • 1 3/4 cup of flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 4 oz hershey chocolate

Steps

  1. In a stove on low heat, heat butter until golden brown
  2. Take off heat, add in gur and sugar and mix.
  3. Add in egg, egg yolk, and vanilla. Mix and then add flour.
  4. Mix until it is about to be fully mixed. Add in half of the chopped hershey chocolate.
  5. Roll cookie dough into similarly sized dough balls. Using tablespoon or melon ball scooper helps.
  6. Put dough balls in freezer for 30 minutes.
  7. Bake in oven at 350 for 12 minutes.
  8. Once out of oven place some of the chopped hershey chocolate on top of each cookie. Let cool before eating.
