Honey Cake Recipe

Description

Honey cake is cake where the center point is honey! Winnie the Pooh would eat this cake up!

This recipe was adapted from Preppy Kitchen on Youtube. The Honey Cake is (in this case) a greek dish served with a drizzle of honey.

Ingredients

For cake
  • 1 cup of all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup of light brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup of crushed jaggery/gur
  • 1/3 cup of honey
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
For optional glaze on top
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon melted butter
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 2 tablespoon milk

Steps

  1. For cake: Mix together flour, baking powder, and cinnamon
  2. In a mxing bowl, add in softened butter, brown sugar, and crushed jaggery. Mix until light and fluffy.
