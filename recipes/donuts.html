Creme Brulee Donut

Description

Image was found from Moribyan.

Creme Brulee Donuts are one of the hardest recipes to ever make! They are perfect for ASMR tho :)

Recipe also adapted from Moribyan.

Ingredients

Donut Dough
  • 3 2/3 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 1/4 cup of milk
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 6 tablespoon of unsalted butter
  • oil for frying
Custard
  • 1 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla bean paste
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 2 tablespoon unsalted butter
Brulee
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water

Steps

  1. Custard: In a mixing bowl, start by mixing egg yolks, cornstarch, sugar, and salt.
  2. In a stove pan, mix milk and vanilla. Heat until you see bubbles at the edges.
  3. Slowly add milk to egg mixture and then pour the egg mixture into pan and heat until thickened! Cool in fridge.
  4. Donut Dough: In a large mixing bowl, add warm milk, yeast, salt, sugar, and egg and mix. Then add butter and flour.
  5. Knead dough for 2-3 minutes and transfer to oiled bowl. Cover and let it sit for the whole night.
  6. Roll out dough and use a cookie cutter to cut out shapes until no more dough is left.
  7. Fry raw donuts in oil at 325F.
  8. Once fried place on wire rack and repeat till all dough has been fried.
  9. Use a piping bag fitted with a small tip, and add in custard. Poke hole in donut and fill with piping bag.
  10. For Brulee: (THIS WAS SO HARD) To a pan add in sugar and water and put on heat until it is golden brown. Do not stir or touch until then.
  11. Dip donuts. Note: Try not to burn fingers off.
  12. Enjoy!
